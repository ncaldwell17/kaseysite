About Barbie


A Los Angeles artist making colorful artwork that brings joy and happiness to your life!
Barbie has been creating art since childhood—she had a painting that toured the world at age 5—and has taken art classes from various institutions including the Toledo Museum of Art and Otis College of Art and Design. Her portfolio is eclectic and bright, working across various mediums and forms: painting, welding, collage, functional furniture, and found objects. She loves the challenge of  making something new and unique.

Barbie’s career path has been equally diverse—with a BA in Finance, she was a financial consultant and real estate broker as well as a golf pro, and a photographer! You can now find her in her studio in Los Angeles, blasting music and transerring inspiration to canvas.

Please reach out to barbierogersgallery@gmail.com if interested in a specific piece, or in commissioning a private work.
